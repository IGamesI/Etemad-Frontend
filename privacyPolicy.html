Etemad Privacy Policy


Etemad is a single sign on service that allows you to access multiple websites with one account. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and share your personal information when you use our service.


What information do we collect?

The only information we collect from you is the information you provide when you sign up for an Etemad account. This may include your name, email address, password and other optional details.


How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide and improve our service, to communicate with you and to protect our service and users. We do not sell, rent or trade your information with any third parties for their own purposes.


How do we share your information?

We only share your information with the websites that you connect to Etemad, and only with your consent. We will show you what type of information will be given to each website before you allow it. You can revoke your consent at any time by disconnecting from a website.


How do we protect your information?

We use encryption and other security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. However, no service is completely secure and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and for logging out of your account when using a shared device.


How can you access or update your information?

You can access and update your information by logging into your Etemad account. However, you cannot edit some of your information, such as your email address, at this time. We are working on adding this feature in the future.


How do we update our privacy policy?

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service or legal requirements. We will not notify you of these changes, so please check this page periodically for the latest version. Your continued use of our service after any changes means that you accept the updated privacy policy.


How can you contact us?

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or our service, please contact us at support@etemad.com. We will respond to your request as soon as possible.
